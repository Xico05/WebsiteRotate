Prologue

We had the pleasure to be responsible for this project, aimed at strengthening the brand image of the Portuguese surfskate manufacturer Bio Boards. The main objective was to make use of our experience and knowledge to deliver unique and efficient solutions to materialize the brand's vision. Here we'll go through all the specified areas we were assigned to develop.

Logo Design

This was a special and interesting aspect of the assignment. We produced a design that connoted a connection between the ground and the water, like surfskating, by drawing influence from the tail of the first ever amphibian known to man. The brand's history is embodied in the new logo, which also adds a contemporary design. The design incorporates three classic boards as the previous logo, which adds a familiar element and calls to mind the history of the company. Overall, the new design successfully conveys what Bio Boards is all about and how they relate to sports and the environment.
