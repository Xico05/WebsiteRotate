Maran

Ongoing

Prologue

We had the pleasure of collaborating with Maran, a well-known Portuguese producer of high quality stainless steel furniture. With our knowledge, we intended to draw attention to the great craftsmanship and distinctive aspects of Maran's furniture range. We were keen to make Maran stand out visually, from a brand image viewpoint as well, catching the soul of the firm and bringing them closer to potential customers.

3D Rendering and Modeling

By using 3D rendering, we were able to create excellent digital images of each product, giving them a unified and expert appearance. This way, Maran was able to cut costs by not having to manufacture actual prototypes just for product photography thanks to 3D rendering. Resulting in Maran being able to showcase visually appealing catalogs while spending less money on manufacturing.

Photography

We portrayed Maran's distinctive manufacturing philosophy while exhibiting its cutting-edge methods and technology. Our images emphasized the tight-knit group of workers, underlining their commitment and passion for delivering top-notch products. To promote a sense of connection and trust with customers, we captured what we thought reflected Maran's brand identity and principles, while emphasizing the craftsmanship and attention to detail that went into each piece of furniture.

Film Making

From the initial concept to the finished piece of furniture, we followed Maran's manufacturing process, highlighting and moving along every step of their production line. Through video, we were able to provide customers with a behind-the-scenes glimpse at the artistry and attention to detail that goes into each item.

Motion Graphics

We created a captivating logo animation for Maran to use as the beginning or conclusion of their videos using motion graphics. The animation highlights the craftsmanship and attention to detail that goes into each piece of furniture by translating into motion graphics the numerous steps in the manufacturing process. It stressed Maran's distinctive production strategy and dedication to quality and innovation.

Epilogue

In conclusion, our services demonstrated Maran's dedication to quality and creativity while providing their clients with a memorable brand experience. It was an honor to be a part of their journey and we look forward to keep helping them succeed in the future.
